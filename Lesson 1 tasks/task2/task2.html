Cheeseburger Soup

In a large saucepan over medium heat, cook and crumble beef until no longer pink,
6-8 minutes; drain and set aside. In same saucepan, melt 1 tablespoon butter over
medium heat. Saute onion, carrots, celery, basil and parsley until vegetables are
tender, about 10 minutes. Add potatoes, broth and ground beef; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat; simmer, covered, until potatoes are tender, 10-12 minutes.
Meanwhile, in a small skillet, melt remaining 3 tablespoons butter. Add flour; cook
and stir until bubbly, 3-5 minutes. Add to soup; bring to a boil. Cook and stir 2
minutes. Reduce heat to low. Stir in cheese, milk, salt and pepper; cook until cheese
melts. Remove from heat; blend in sour cream. If desired, serve with onion rings and
green onions

supermarket list

  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 4 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 3/4 cup chopped onion
  • 3/4 cup shredded carrots
